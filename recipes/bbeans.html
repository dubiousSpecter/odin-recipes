Baked Beans

Description

Want some Baked Beans? Here's how to make Baked Beans.

Ingredients

  • Baked Beans
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Dump beans into pot.
  2. Bring beans to gentle boil.
  3. Stir in salt and pepper to taste, allowing mixture to simmer gently for two minutes.
  4. Serve hot. Enjoy!
