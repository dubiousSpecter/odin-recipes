Fried Eggs

Description

Want some fried eggs? Here's how to make fried eggs.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the pan.
  2. Add a tablespoon of oil to the pan.
  3. Break eggs into the pan.
  4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Once the whites have hardened, it's good to go. Enjoy!
  6. If you want to harden up that yolk, turn off the heat and flip the egg, allowing the residual heat in the pan to cook the yolk.
